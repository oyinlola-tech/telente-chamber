Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from Okitipupa clients.

We want you to feel informed and confident before engaging our team.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond within 24 hours for new inquiries. Urgent matters can be escalated through the emergency contact line.

Do you offer consultations in Okitipupa?

Yes. We offer confidential consultations in Okitipupa and can also schedule virtual meetings when needed.

How do you structure fees?

We provide clear fee estimates after an initial review. Every matter includes a written scope and timeline.

Can you help with land title verification?

Absolutely. Our team handles title verification, boundary disputes, and land documentation across Ondo State.

Do you represent businesses and cooperatives?

Yes. We advise SMEs, family enterprises, and cooperatives on contracts, compliance, and dispute management.

Will I work directly with a senior counsel?

Yes. Senior counsel leads all engagements, supported by a dedicated team for research and documentation.
