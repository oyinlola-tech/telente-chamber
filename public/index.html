About Telente Chambers

Founded on the principles of justice, integrity, and excellence, Telente Chambers has been serving clients with distinction for over two decades. Our firm was established with a clear vision: to provide exceptional legal services while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Our team of experienced attorneys specializes in various areas of law, including corporate law, intellectual property, civil litigation, and family law. Each attorney brings unique expertise and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients.

At Telente Chambers, we believe that every client deserves personalized attention and strategic legal solutions. We take the time to understand your unique circumstances and develop tailored approaches to address your legal needs effectively.

Our track record of success is built on thorough preparation, innovative thinking, and a deep understanding of the law. We pride ourselves on being not just legal representatives, but trusted advisors who guide our clients through complex legal challenges with confidence and clarity.

Our Founding Lawyer

Alexandra M. Telente, Esq.

Founding Partner & Senior Counsel

With over 25 years of legal experience, Alexandra M. Telente founded Telente Chambers in 2005 with a vision to create a law firm that combines exceptional legal expertise with unwavering client commitment.

A graduate of Harvard Law School (J.D., 1995), Ms. Telente began her career at one of New York's premier corporate law firms before establishing her own practice. She specializes in corporate law, intellectual property litigation, and complex civil disputes.
